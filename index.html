Physical GOLD

On September 11, 2001, more than $1 billion worth of gold and silver was stored in vaults beneath the World Trade Center.

After the attacks, most of it disappeared.

Roughly 200 tons of precious metals were never recovered.

Some trucks were seen leaving the site before the collapse.

There was no full investigation, no media follow-up, and no clear explanation.

To this day, the missing gold remains unaccounted for.
